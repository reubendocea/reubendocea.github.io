Curriculum Vitae Highlights

Throughout my adult life, I have pursued promising avenues for positive societal impact and personal growth, with success in diverse contexts: from establishing a university society to 3D-print prosthetic devices for children in Glasgow, to working on low-footprint robotic agriculture, and researching at the interface of computer vision and minimally invasive surgery. This ability to identify impactful future trends was highlighted at a recent lab retreat, where my presentation on the significance of broader AI developments for my work was recognised as the "most inspiring talk". As my time at the German Cancer Research Center concludes, I am eagerly seeking to join a new initiative with a clear and compelling vision in this period of transformation. For the right role and project, I offer hard work, enthusiasm, team spirit, initiative, and dedication.

ROS-based Surgical Navigation Integration

Led the ROS-based integration of a navigation system for minimally invasive liver surgery, with a focus on SLAM (public code repository). See this video demonstration.

Surgical Data Collection & 4D Reconstruction

Advanced Robotics Vision Systems

3D-Printed Prosthetics & Community
