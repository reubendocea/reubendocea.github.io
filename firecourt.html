The Fire Court

During Aberdeen's 2020 lockdown, I transformed granite stones from our house extension into a circular drywall court with a central cheminea. This meditative building project became a gathering place that brought friends together around fire during a time of isolation. Scroll to see the journey from clearing to completion.
